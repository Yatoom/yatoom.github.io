Quick Guide

How to Play

Complete colors and columns to score. The session ends when a player finishes 2 full colors — highest score wins.

01

Roll & Combine

Six dice are rolled: three colors and three numbers. Pick one of each to mark cells on your personal grid.

4
✕
→ 4 cells
02

Connect from Center

Your first mark must touch the center column (H). After that, every new mark must be orthogonally adjacent to your existing color cluster.

03

Score Big

Be the first to complete a color for 5 points (others get 3). Complete columns first for higher rewards and capture moons for +2 bonus points.

04

Jokers & Penalties

Stuck? Use a joker to pick any color or number. They are limited, and each use costs 1 point — use them wisely.
