Why Luma Luma?

Everything you love about classic dice strategy, enhanced for mobile.

Infinite Procedural Grids

Our algorithm generates a fresh, balanced board for every match. No two games are ever the same, forcing you to adapt your strategy constantly.

Smart AI Opponents

Practice against bots that actually understand strategy. Multiple difficulty levels from beginner to ruthless grid master.

Seamless Multiplayer

No accounts required for casual play. Share a room via QR code or link.

Day & Night Ready

Full dark mode support that respects your system preference. Easy on the eyes for late-night strategy sessions.

Landscape Optimized

Rotate your device for a dedicated landscape view. See the grid in full detail, making it easier to mark cells and plan your strategy on both phones and tablets.

One player creates a room and gets a 6-digit code plus a QR image. Others join by scanning the code or copying the digits. The grid is generated once and synchronized to all devices in real time.

Luma Luma requires Android 13 (API 33) or newer. It runs smoothly on phones and tablets, including foldable devices. Plus, it features a dedicated landscape mode for a bigger, more accessible grid view.
