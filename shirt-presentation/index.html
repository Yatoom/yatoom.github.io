Design process

Things we thought of

  • A tag like zombie game
  • A rock-paper-scissor like team-game with powerups
  • An interactive shirt that responds to your movements

The solutions we came up with

  • Shirt has a personality
  • With different emotions...

Sleepy

  • A calm blue color.
  • Shirt is 'sleeping'.

Happy

  • A cheerful green color.
  • Makes cheerful bleeps.
  • Shirt is 'happy'.

Angry

  • A malicous red color.
  • Makes angry bleeps.
  • Shirt is 'angry'.

Bored

  • A neutral white color.
  • Dull sound effect.
  • Shirt is 'bored'.

Next, we added a set of rules

  • Shirt likes to be active.
  • A sudden stop makes the shirt angry.
  • Too much motion can tire out the shirt.
  • Not moving for a long time makes the shirt bored.
  • Falls asleep after a while.

Aim of the design

The demographic we aim for

  • Adolescents and children
  • Active and curious stance towards the world

Our goal

  • Invite curiosity as well as activity
  • Provide a fun activator for the user to play and interact with
  • Play around with emotinal states and see if they cause behavior change
