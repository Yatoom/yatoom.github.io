Sleepy

  • A calm blue color.
  • Shirt is 'sleeping'.

Happy

  • A cheerful green color.
  • Makes cheerful bleeps.
  • Shirt is 'happy'.

Angry

  • A malicous red color.
  • Makes angry bleeps.
  • Shirt is 'angry'.

Bored

  • A neutral white color.
  • Dull sound effect.
  • Shirt is 'bored'.

Set of rules.

  • Shirt likes to be active.
  • A sudden stop makes the shirt angry.
  • Too much motion can tire out the shirt.
  • Not moving for a long time makes the shirt bored.
  • Falls asleep after a while.
