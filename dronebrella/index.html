The System

The Drone Brella has a large fan to make sure you have a stable roof above your head, even in the toughest huricane. The round edge around the fan contains weights which can be moved, so that the Drone Brella always faces the right direction at the right angle. The screen that keeps you dry is seated below the fan. The fan and the screen are connected via a rod (not visible in the model). The screen has glowing blue lights underneath that simulate the dailight sky. This will take the gray atmosphere away and light your path when the dark clouds block the sun light.

You can call a Drone Brella whenever you want via the site, or with our Drone Brella App. It will then dispatch from the nearest Drone Brella garage and use GPS to fly at a safe hight in a straight line to your location, and arrive in less than 15 minutes. The Drone Brella will wait outside until you are ready to go. If you no longer wish to use the Drone Brella, or the weather clears up, it will automatically fly back to its garage.
